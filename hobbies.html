Biking

"I first liked riding a bicycle in 2008 or 2009."

My current bicycle is a Fixed Gear, or more commonly called as "Fixie".

I started riding this kind of bike since 2017, and then as time went by I liked the fact that I can go and maintain a high speed with it.

That's when I started making its build into a track bike build, I had to work to get money in doing so.

When it became a bike that is able to go with the cyclist at Mall of Asia, I finally went there to train my cardio and my lower muscles' endurance. Although I did not frequently trained, it still significantly strengthened those mentioned.
